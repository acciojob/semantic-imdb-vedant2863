Inception

A science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan.
Actors | Quotes
Inception is a story about Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from people's minds by entering their dreams. He is given a chance to erase his criminal history by performing an impossible task called inception — planting an idea into someone's mind instead of stealing it.
Main actors in the movie include Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, and Ellen Page as Ariadne. Their performances bring depth to the complex dream-within-a-dream story.
One of the most memorable quotes from the movie is: "You mustn't be afraid to dream a little bigger, darling."
